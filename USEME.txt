- Click on the "Load Image" button to load an image
from the computer.
- Once loaded, click on any of the buttons to perform
the action. The action will always be performed on the
current image being shown.
- When an action is performed, a pop-up will show,
prompting for the new name of the image.
- To change which image is being currently worked on,
click on the image name in the list in the bottom.
